About Osu!

Osu! is a rhythm game where players click circles, slide sliders, and spin spinners to the beat of music. The goal is to hit targets accurately and in time with the song to earn points and achieve a high score. It's a test of rhythm, precision, and hand-eye coordination!

Note: This PICO-8 version currently focuses on the core tapping mechanics. Audio, complex sliders, and spinners are not yet implemented. You can adjust Approach Rate (AR) and Overall Difficulty (OD) in the game's internal options to customize your challenge!
